About Me

I am a music composer, arranger and overall producer from Italy, based in Tokyo.

My specialization in the field is “Background Music Production”, with major skills and experience in the videogame industry.

I also play electric bass, which I started learning at the age of 15. After that, I started taking interest in music composition, specifically in compiling trackers for Chiptune Music Production.

I then became proficient in NES (Nintendo Entertainment System) trackers and started composing music on them.

At the age of 19, I flew to Japan where I studied Japanese for about a year, and soon after that I began studying Music Production in Japan. I graduated in 2018 and started working as a freelancer soon after.
